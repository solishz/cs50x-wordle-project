How to Play Wordle:

  1. Guess the Word: You have six chances to guess the hidden five-letter word.
  2. Enter Your Guess: Type a five-letter word and press "Enter" to submit.
  3. Color Feedback:
    • Green letters are correct and in the right position.
    • Yellow letters are in the word but in the wrong position.
    • Gray letters are not in the word at all.
  4. Use Hints: Use the feedback from each guess to refine your next attempt.
  5. Win or Lose: If you guess the word within six tries, you win! If not, better luck next time!

Good luck, and have fun!
